Destinations

Alps

The Alps are renowned for their majestic beauty and diverse recreational opportunities. With picturesque landscapes, alpine villages. In the summer, it transforms into a hiker's paradise, with trails catering to all skill levels. The region also has stunning lakes, such as Lake Geneva, adding to its allure.

Himalayas

The Himalayas, world's highest mountain range. With its snow-capped peaks, serene valleys, and diverse cultural heritage, it offers an unforgettable experience. Mount Everest, the highest peak on Earth, allures mountaineers, while trekking routes like the Annapurna Circuit and Everest Base Camp Trek captivate adventure seekers.

Andes

Andes, shared by Argentina and Chile, is a captivating mountain region in South America. With its majestic peaks, expansive glaciers, and turquoise lakes, it offers a breathtaking landscape. Visitors can explore the renowned Torres del Paine National Park, marvel at the Perito Moreno Glacier, or Tierra del Fuego archipelago.

Carpathians

The Carpathians, located in Eastern Europe. This ancient mountain range offers unspoiled wilderness, traditional villages, and cultural heritage. Hikers can explore the stunning landscapes of the Carpathian National Nature Parks, while immersing themselves in the region's folklore and rustic traditions.

Annapurna

Annapurna, Nepali Annapurna Himal, massif of the Himalayas in north-central Nepal. Annapurna I is the world's 10th highest peak.

Info

  • Challenge yourself to one of the most scenic and challenging treks in the world.
  • See cascading waterfalls, beautiful villages,amazing mountain vistas, and more.
  • Visit the Gurung musuem on Ghandruk and learn about the culture and history.
  • See impressive high peaks like Machhapuchhre, Annapurna south, Annapurna I and more.
  • Be guided from your patient, knowledgeable English-speaking guide.

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu, site of ancient Inca ruins located about 80 km northwest of Cuzco, Peru, in the Cordillera de Vilcabamba of the Andes Mountains.

Info

  • Explore the Sacred Valley and Machu Picchu on this 2-day tour from Cusco.
  • Visit the archaeological sites of Pisac, Urubamba, and Ollantaytambo.
  • Watch the sunrise over the famous Machu Picchu and learn about the Incas.
  • Breakfast and hotel accommodations included.
  • Comfortable round-trip transport by bus and train.

Saxer Lücke

This Saxer Lücke trail is located in Eastern Switzerland, near the border of Liechtenstein. It is part of the Alpstein mountain range in the Appenzell region.

Info

  • Visit the best viewpoints to see famous peaks and glacier summits.
  • Travel with a guide for easy logistics, but have free time at each stop.
  • Explore Saxer Lücke and enjoy ample free time for lunch (own expense).
  • Stay in Saxer Lücke longer if you wish, and return to Geneva at a later time.
